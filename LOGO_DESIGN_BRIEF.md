# Logo Design Brief for Mahdi Ahmed Hasan Al-Sheikh Contracting Est

## COMPANY INFORMATION

**Company Name:** Mahdi Ahmed Hasan Al-Sheikh Contracting Est  
**Tagline/Subtitle:** "Manpower & Heavy Equipments Consultancy"  
**Full Tagline:** "Your trusted partner for professional manpower solutions and heavy equipment rental services"  
**Location:** Saudi Arabia  
**Industry:** Construction, Manpower Services, Heavy Equipment Rental, Contracting

## BUSINESS OVERVIEW

### Core Services:
1. **Manpower Services / Skilled & Unskilled Services**
   - Technical & Skilled Workers (Electricians, Welders, Plumbers, Carpenters, etc.)

   - General & Office Staff (Labor, Admin, Document Controllers, Secretaries)

2. **Equipment Rental Services**
   - Construction & Industrial Equipment (Loaders, Excavators, Cranes, Forklifts, Generators, Compressors)
   - Vehicles & Tankers (Trucks, Dump Trucks, Concrete Mixers, Water Tankers, Manlifts)

3. **Professional Support Services**
   - Engineering & Management (Project Managers, Engineers, Quantity Surveyors)
   - QA/QC & Inspection Services
   - Supervision & Safety (HSE Managers, Safety Officers, Supervisors, Foremen)

### Brand Values:
- **Excellence** - Committed to delivering excellence in every project

### Target Audience:

- Engineering firms
- Project managers
- Government contractors
- Large-scale project developers
- Manufacturing companies

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Style Guidelines:
- **Style:** Modern, Professional, Corporate, Industrial
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, sophisticated, trustworthy, robust
- **Tone:** Professional yet approachable, strong and reliable
- **Feel:** Should convey construction/industrial expertise while maintaining modern appeal

### Design Elements to Consider:
- Construction/industrial imagery (hard hats, tools, buildings, equipment)
- Geometric shapes that suggest strength and stability
- Professional badge or shield motifs (representing trust and certification)
- Modern typography that balances professionalism with readability
- Can incorporate subtle references to construction, manpower, or heavy equipment

### Logo Variations Needed:
- **Full Logo:** Icon + Company Name + Tagline (horizontal layout)
- **Icon Only:** Standalone symbol/mark
- **Wordmark:** Company name with integrated icon elements
- **Compact Version:** For smaller spaces (mobile, favicon, etc.)

---

## TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### File Formats:
- Vector format (SVG, AI, EPS) for scalability
- High-resolution PNG with transparent background
- Multiple sizes (small, medium, large)
- Favicon versions (16x16, 32x32, 64x64, 128x128)

### Usage Context:
- Website header/navigation
- Business cards and letterheads
- Vehicle signage
- Construction site signage
- Email signatures
- Social media profiles
- Mobile app (if applicable)
- Print materials (brochures, flyers, reports)
- Digital presentations

### Design Constraints:
- Must work on both light and dark backgrounds
- Should be legible at small sizes (minimum 24px height)
- Should scale well for large applications (billboards, vehicle wraps)
- Must maintain readability when printed in single color (for certain applications)
- Should work well in both horizontal and vertical layouts

## FINAL NOTES

The logo should immediately communicate:
1. **Professionalism** - This is a serious, established business
2. **Industry Expertise** - Construction, manpower, and heavy equipment
3. **Trust & Reliability** - A partner you can depend on
4. **Modern Approach** - Contemporary methods and technology
5. **Quality Focus** - Excellence in service delivery

The design should be distinctive enough to be memorable while remaining professional and appropriate for corporate and industrial contexts. It should work equally well on a business card, a construction site banner, a website header, and corporate letterhead.
