# Logo Design AI Prompt

Use this prompt when working with AI logo design tools or agents:

---

## PROMPT FOR AI LOGO DESIGN AGENT

**Create a professional, modern logo for a construction and manpower consultancy company with the following specifications:**

### Company Details:
- **Name:** Mahdi Ahmed Hasan Al-Sheikh Contracting Est
- **Tagline:** "Manpower & Heavy Equipments Consultancy"
- **Industry:** Construction, Manpower Services, Heavy Equipment Rental
- **Location:** Saudi Arabia

### Business Focus:
The company provides:
1. Skilled manpower solutions (electricians, welders, operators, technicians, engineers)
2. Heavy equipment rental (cranes, excavators, loaders, generators, trucks)
3. Professional support services (project management, QA/QC, safety supervision)

### Brand Values:
Excellence, Expert Team, Trusted Partner, Quality Service

### Color Requirements:
- **Primary:** Deep Blue (#1e3a8a) - Professional, trustworthy, stable
- **Accent:** Amber/Gold (#f59e0b) - Energy, quality, premium
- **Secondary:** Bright Blue (#3b82f6) - Modern, reliable
- Should work on both light and dark backgrounds

### Design Style:
- **Style:** Modern, Professional, Corporate, Industrial
- **Mood:** Trustworthy, reliable, expert, modern, strong
- **Feel:** Professional yet approachable, clean and sophisticated
- Avoid: Cartoonish, overly aggressive, or cliché construction imagery

### Logo Requirements:
- Must include icon/symbol + company name + tagline
- Should incorporate construction/industrial elements (hard hat, tools, building, equipment)
- Consider professional badge or shield motifs (representing trust and certification)
- Modern typography (clean sans-serif, professional)
- Geometric shapes that suggest strength and stability
- Work in horizontal and vertical layouts
- Must be scalable (from favicon to billboard size)
- Professional gradient usage (subtle, not flashy)

### Deliverables Needed:
- Full logo (icon + text + tagline)
- Icon/symbol only
- Horizontal layout
- Vertical/stacked layout
- Monochrome version
- Color variations
- Multiple sizes

### Target Audience:
Construction companies, industrial firms, engineering companies, project managers, government contractors, large-scale developers

### Key Message:
"The logo should immediately communicate: Professionalism, Industry Expertise, Trust & Reliability, Modern Approach, and Quality Focus. It should be distinctive yet appropriate for corporate and industrial contexts."

## DETAILED DESIGN DIRECTION (For Advanced AI Tools)

**Create a professional logo design that:**

1. **Icon/Symbol Design:**
   - Incorporate construction/industrial imagery (hard hat, building, tools, equipment, or combination)
   - Use geometric shapes suggesting strength and stability (shield, badge, hexagon, or modern abstract form)
   - Consider layered elements or modern iconography
   - Should be recognizable at small sizes (minimum 24px height)

2. **Typography:**
   - Primary name: "Mahdi Ahmed Hasan Al-Sheikh Contracting Est" - bold, professional sans-serif
   - Tagline: "Manpower & Heavy Equipments Consultancy" - lighter weight, uppercase or all caps
   - Font should complement the icon (suggestions: Poppins, Montserrat, Inter, or similar modern sans-serif)
   - Consider custom letterforms for uniqueness

3. **Color Application:**
   - Primary: Deep Blue (#1e3a8a) for main text and icon base
   - Accent: Amber/Gold (#f59e0b) for highlights, badges, or key elements
   - Gradient: Subtle gradient from deep blue to bright blue (#3b82f6) if appropriate
   - Ensure sufficient contrast for readability

4. **Layout Options:**
   - **Horizontal:** Icon left, text right (for headers, banners)
   - **Vertical/Stacked:** Icon top, text below (for square spaces, social media)
   - **Compact:** Icon + abbreviated name (for small spaces)

5. **Design Elements:**
   - Subtle shadows or depth effects (professional, not 3D heavy)
   - Clean lines and shapes
   - Balance between modern and traditional
   - Professional badge/seal elements if appropriate
   - Consider subtle pattern or texture (very minimal)

6. **Avoid:**
   - Overly complex designs
   - Cliché construction tools (unless uniquely stylized)
   - Playful or cartoon styles
   - Aggressive or harsh aesthetics
   - Too many colors (stick to 2-3 main colors)
